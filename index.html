THE BIO

I'm a student at Bryan Collegiate High School where I was recently honored as Student of the Month! I'm known for being fast and active—I don't sit still for long.

My life is built on energy, faith, and the outdoors. I spent years in Trail Life Troop 12, a national scouting organization that taught me how to survive and thrive in the woods. One of my favorite memories was an annual summer camping trip at a Colorado State Park where our troop was actually featured on the news in 2022!
